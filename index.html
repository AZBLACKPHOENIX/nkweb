Our Journey

The Beginning: 2017

In 2017, a young and vibrant Nkosinathi Mdlovu embarked on his career at one of South Africa's fastest-growing banks. Starting as a Client Service Champion, he was the first point of contact for clients, greeting them warmly at the branch entrance. His primary responsibility was to understand their needs and direct them to the appropriate services, ensuring a seamless banking experience.


Nkosinathi's dedication to his role and his positive attitude quickly set him apart. His commitment to excellence not only earned him the trust of clients but also caught the attention of his superiors. This early recognition was a testament to his potential and laid the foundation for his rapid rise within the company.

Rising Through the Ranks: 2017-2018

With a passion for service and a drive to excel, Nkosinathi Mdlovu was promoted to a Service Consultant within just nine months. This swift advancement highlighted his growing expertise and commitment to the banking industry. In this new role, Nkosinathi provided personalized banking solutions, skillfully addressing a wide range of financial needs across diverse client demographics.

His ability to connect with clients on a personal level and deliver exceptional service became a hallmark of his work during this period. Nkosinathi's dedication to understanding each client's unique situation and offering tailored advice not only solidified his reputation but also contributed to the bank's continued success.

Gaining Experience: 2018-2023

Over the next five years, Nkosinathi Mdlovu gained invaluable experience in the banking industry. He interacted with clients from diverse racial, age, and financial backgrounds, mastering the art of banking and customer service. This period was instrumental in shaping his expertise, as he honed his skills in addressing the unique needs of each client.

Nkosinathi's journey was marked by continuous learning and a deepening understanding of the financial landscape. His commitment to personal and professional growth allowed him to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry, positioning himself as a trusted advisor to clients and a valuable asset to the bank.

After gaining substantial experience and establishing a solid reputation in the banking sector, Nkosinathi decided to take the bold step of resigning from his position. His decision was driven by a vision to create something greater Aid Finance, a new company set to evolve into a fully-fledged bank. His goal with Aid Finance is to offer innovative financial solutions and unparalleled services, aiming to redefine the banking experience for individuals and businesses alike.

The Vision for the Future: 2023 and Beyond

Inspired by his experiences and the knowledge he had acquired over the years, Nkosinathi Mdlovu realized his true calling in the banking industry. He envisioned creating a financial institution that would not only serve clients but also empower them to achieve their financial goals. This vision marked the beginning of a new chapter in his career, where he would take on the challenge of building something truly impactful.

Driven by commitment to excellence and innovation, Nkosinathi set out to bring his dream to life. His journey toward establishing his own bank was fueled by a desire to offer more than just traditional banking services. He aimed to create a platform that would provide clients with the tools and knowledge needed to take control of their financial futures, ultimately transforming the way banking is perceived and experienced.

Introducing Aid Finance

A registered credit provider NCRCP17839. We offer financial solutions in form of loans delivered online, our branches and WhatsApp line to deliver a seamless experience to our clients across the entire South Africa. We have a vision to establish the first ever fully people-centric bank in South Africa, which will spread its wings to the Southern part of Africa in 5 to 10 years and reach the whole of Africa in 15 to 20 years, making an international mark the entire world in 25 years.

We believe that banks don't have money at all, but only manage entities and people's money. This is why banks should put people first and create banking products that are suitable for their clients and have impactful and transformative socio-investment programs to give back and serve the society.

Nkosinathi Mdlovu, the CEO of Aid Finance, is the brains behind this visionary idea. As the leader of the new upcoming bank, Nkosinathi is dedicated to transforming the banking landscape with a focus on community-centric solutions and innovative financial services. Aid Finance is committed to providing tailored investment solutions to help you achieve your financial goals while making a positive impact on society.
