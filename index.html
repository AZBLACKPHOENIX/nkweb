Our Journey

The Beginning: 2017

In 2017, a young and vibrant Nkosinathi Ndlovu embarked on his career at one of South Africa's fastest-growing banks. Starting as a Client Service Champion, he was the first point of contact for clients, greeting them warmly at the branch entrance. His primary responsibility was to understand their needs and direct them to the appropriate services, ensuring a seamless banking experience.


Nkosinathi's dedication to his role and his positive attitude quickly set him apart. His commitment to excellence not only earned him the trust of clients but also caught the attention of his superiors. This early recognition was a testament to his potential and laid the foundation for his rapid rise within the company.

Rising Through the Ranks: 2017-2018

With a passion for service and a drive to excel, Nkosinathi Ndlovu was promoted to a Service Consultant within just nine months. This swift advancement highlighted his growing expertise and commitment to the banking industry. In this new role, Nkosinathi provided personalized banking solutions, skillfully addressing a wide range of financial needs across diverse client demographics.

His ability to connect with clients on a personal level and deliver exceptional service became a hallmark of his work during this period. Nkosinathi's dedication to understanding each client's unique situation and offering tailored advice not only solidified his reputation but also contributed to the bank's continued success.

Gaining Experience: 2018-2023

Over the next five years, Nkosinathi Ndlovu gained invaluable experience in the banking industry. He interacted with clients from diverse racial, age, and financial backgrounds, mastering the art of banking and customer service. This period was instrumental in shaping his expertise, as he honed his skills in addressing the unique needs of each client.

Nkosinathi's journey was marked by continuous learning and a deepening understanding of the financial landscape. His commitment to personal and professional growth allowed him to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry, positioning himself as a trusted advisor to clients and a valuable asset to the bank.

After gaining substantial experience and establishing a solid reputation in the banking sector, Nkosinathi decided to take the bold step of resigning from his position. His decision was driven by a vision to create something greater Aid Finance, a new company set to evolve into a fully-fledged bank. His goal with Aid Finance is to offer innovative financial solutions and unparalleled services, aiming to redefine the banking experience for individuals and businesses alike.

The Vision for the Future: 2023 and Beyond

Inspired by his experiences and the knowledge he had acquired over the years, Nkosinathi Ndlovu realized his true calling in the banking industry. He envisioned creating a financial institution that would not only serve clients but also empower them to achieve their financial goals. This vision marked the beginning of a new chapter in his career, where he would take on the challenge of building something truly impactful.

Driven by commitment to excellence and innovation, Nkosinathi set out to bring his dream to life. His journey toward establishing his own bank was fueled by a desire to offer more than just traditional banking services. He aimed to create a platform that would provide clients with the tools and knowledge needed to take control of their financial futures, ultimately transforming the way banking is perceived and experienced.

Micro Lending

How It Works

1. Application

Check how much you could qualify for, what you could pay a month, and when happy with the quote, just click apply. The application is simple, fast, and fully online.

2. Documentation & Verification

Fill in your personal and financial information, including ID, proof of income, bank statement, and proof of residence. We will verify your information to assess creditworthiness and eligibility.

3. Credit Assessment

Our credit department will assess your creditworthiness, credit bureau details, and affordability. Feedback will be provided within 24 business hours.

4. Loan Approval

If you meet our lending criteria and pass the credit assessment, your loan will be approved and you will receive a DebiCheck mandate to approve, upon approving the DebiCheck mandate your loan will paid into your bank account within 24hours.
